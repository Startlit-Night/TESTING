Reminiscence

Childhood is a beautiful part of your life. This is when you feel excited to go outside and play with your friends joyously. All you ever focused on was wanting to find that one sneaky child in your hide-and-seek game, or that athletic and annoying kid whose movements were so swift that you wouldn't be able to tag him/her even when he/she stood in his/her place for awhile. It was frustrating, wasn't it? And although it felt like that, these emotions still put a smile on your face as you think back to those sweet, happy memories.
